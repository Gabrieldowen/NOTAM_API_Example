Overview
Our project aims to develop a user-friendly NOTAM (Notice to Airmen) display system tailored for pilots. NOTAMs are crucial for pilots as they contain important information about potential hazards, changes, or other factors affecting flight safety. However, the current methods of accessing NOTAMs can be cumbersome and time-consuming. Our system seeks to streamline this process and provide pilots with a more efficient way to access and interpret NOTAMs.
